How outbound callbacks work

When the chat AI detects a customer needs a callback, it collects their Name and Phone, then initiates an AI Voice call — carrying the full chat context so the voice agent continues seamlessly. The same system prompt powers both inbound and outbound calls.

How inbound call forwarding works

Business owners forward their phone to your shared SuperOwl DID. When a customer calls, Vapi reads the SIP Diversion header to identify which business it is, then starts a personalised agent using that business's config.

🎙️ Inbound Welcome Message
What the AI says when it answers an inbound call.
Variables: {{businessName}}, {{city}}, {{hours}} — filled from the ANI registry per business.
